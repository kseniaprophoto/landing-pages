Meet Ksenia

Photographer · Artist · Mother

With 15 years of portrait and fashion photography experience and over 1,000 sessions delivered, Ksenia built her luxury studio from scratch after immigrating to the United States — driven by the belief that every mother deserves images as extraordinary as the journey she's on.

As a mother herself, she knows how fast these moments pass. "It seems like my daughter was born yesterday, and now I put her on a school bus every morning." That's why she photographs every maternity session with the same urgency and artistry she'd want for her own family.
